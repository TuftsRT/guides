# Beginner's guide for conducting bioinformatics analysis on Tufts HPC

## Welcome

The goals of this workshop are to:

- Help users get started with bioinformatics analysis, especially command-line bioinformatics, on Tufts HPC.
- Familiarize with bioinformatics resources/tools available on Tufts HPC.

## Prerequisites

[Access to Tufts HPC](https://shib-idp.tufts.edu/idp/profile/SAML2/POST/SSO?execution=e1s1) (**Note:** To test your account, log into [OnDemand](https://ondemand.pax.tufts.edu/). If you can log in, you are all set, if not please reach out to {{ email }})
